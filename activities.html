Activities at Pacific Trails

Hiking

Pacific Trails Resort has 5 miles of hiking trails and is adjacent to a state park. Go it alone or join one of our guided hikes.

Kayaking

Ocean kayaks are available for guest use. Sign up for lessons available as an add on through our Kayak Away package.

Bird Watching

While anytime is a good time for bird watching at Pacific Trails, we offer guided bird watching trips at sunrise several times a week.
