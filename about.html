Our Trail Roots

Our Journey

Pacific Trails resort strives to provide first class hospitality to guests of all ages. We focus on curating a customizable experience with a variety of trails to explore and activities to immerse yourself in. Whether you prefer to relax along the beach after a scenic hike, hit the waves in one of our kayaks, or enjoy the diverse nature; Pacific Trails has something for you.

The vision of Pacific Trails took root from a family who bonded over camping trips in nature. Originally from Northern California, the family traveled with their children and niece by RV, exploring the vast beauty of the American West. Through their adventurous spirit, the idea of Pacific Trails was born.
